We Are!

Song by Hiroshi Kitadani,
Composed by Kohei Tanaka
Lyrics by Shoko Fujibayashi.

It was used as the first opening theme for the One Piece television anime, accompanying most of the episodes comprising the East Blue Saga. As a result, it became a sort of shorthand for the Straw Hat Pirates' earliest adventures (and, among the wider public, for the series as a whole).
